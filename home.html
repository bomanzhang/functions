Cyberfeminist Art

This is a curated collection of cyberfeminist artists and their works or artworks that relate to the cyberfeminist discourse. Its primary purpose is to serve as an invaluable resource for students, artists, and anyone who is curious about the realm of cyberfeminism.



What is cyberfeminism?


The field of cyberfeminism emerged in the early 1990s after the arrival of the world wide web, which went live in August 1991. Its roots, however, go back to the earlier practices of feminist artists like Lynn Hershman Leeson. Cyberfeminism came to describe an international, unofficial group of female thinkers, coders, and media artists who began linking up online.
